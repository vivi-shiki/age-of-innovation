Age of Innovation

Age of Innovation is a strategy game for 2–5 players set in a world of seven different terrain types, each home to a different faction. Players expand their civilization by terraforming the landscape, building structures, advancing on cult tracks, and founding towns.

The game spans six rounds. At the end of each round, players score points based on the active scoring tile. Final scoring rewards network size, cult track positions, and resources.

Players: 2–5  |  Duration: 60–150 min  |  Complexity: High
